How to find us

Address

The museum is located at 1725 Slough Avenue in Scranton Pennsylvania

Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leaves every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person, and include a handy printed guide of the museum, If you would like to organize a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Thursday: 10:00 - 16:00

Saturday: 9:00 - 16:00

Sunday: 09:00 - 13:00

Accessibility

The museum has accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum, as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
